Member of

RSQUARED HOLDING began its journey in 1981 by the guidance of CEO and Founder Eng.Abdel Raouf Hussein by establishing RSQUARED CONSTRUCTION. RSQUARED CONSTRUCTION focused on all kinds of infrastructure projects through out Egypt. After a while RSQUARED CONSTRUCTION concentrated all of its work force on Road construction projects in Egypt, Sudan and Iraq.
