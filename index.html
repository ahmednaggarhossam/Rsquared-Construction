About

Founded in 1981, Rsquared Construction is one of Egypt’s leading privately owned construction and infrastructure companies operating on a stand-alone basis. Our success is derived from focusing on three things; managing time, quality, and cost. Our team of experts thrive to maintain focus on new innovation in most of our projects. RSQUARED CONSTRUCTION is now studying markets in the middle east and gulf area to expand our reach in the whole region. Rsquared executed projects in the past 41 years that were focused on, building construction, road construction, tunnels, sewage work, bridges, concrete roads and much more. We are always challenging ourselves by picking projects outside our previous scope to expand our experience.
